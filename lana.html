Inspired from the 70's

Lana Banana is first of all the nickname of a character from the “American Horror Story” TV show that takes place in 1964. The shop is selling classy but affordable women 70s clothes designed in Italy, and I wanted to give the brand a positive cognitive aspect by focusing on the use of the letter “A”.
